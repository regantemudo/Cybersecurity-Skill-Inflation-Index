Job Description:
HSBC is seeking an IT Auditor Apprentice to join the Global Technology Audit team. This 1-year apprenticeship offers hands-on exposure to IT audit, risk assessment, and compliance activities across the HSBC Group.

Responsibilities:
- Learn to undertake risk assessments and control design reviews of business applications
- Assist with audits of software development and technical infrastructure
- Help provide consultancy on IT strategy, architecture, security risks, and controls
- Draft audit findings articulating issues, risks, root causes, and action plans
- Maintain knowledge of Global Internal Audit standards and best practices

Requirements:
- 0 years experience (entry-level apprenticeship)
- University degree or equivalent qualification
- Keen interest in technology audits and information security
- Good written, verbal, and analytical skills
- Basic understanding of NIST and ISO 27001 frameworks is a plus
- No certification required at entry level
