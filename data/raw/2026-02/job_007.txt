Job Description:
OneAdvanced is seeking a Cyber Security GRC Analyst to support governance, risk, and compliance activities with a focus on cyber security metrics, reporting, policy management, and framework-aligned compliance.

Responsibilities:
- Collect, analyse, and report cyber security metrics
- Identify trends and support discussions on underperformance areas
- Prepare reports and presentations for governance and leadership forums
- Support review and maintenance of cyber security policies and standards
- Support ISO/IEC 27001 implementation and ongoing compliance
- Assist with NIST CSF and SOC 2 compliance activities
- Track audit findings and corrective actions to closure
- Engage stakeholders to explain compliance and reporting requirements

Requirements:
- 3-5 years experience in a Cyber Security GRC, Information Security, Risk, or Compliance role
- Experience supporting ISO 27001 implementation and audits
- Exposure to NIST CSF and SOC 2 frameworks is desirable
- Strong analytical capability and experience with metrics and reporting
- CISA or ISO 27001 Lead Implementer certification preferred
- Proficiency with reporting, documentation, and presentation tools
