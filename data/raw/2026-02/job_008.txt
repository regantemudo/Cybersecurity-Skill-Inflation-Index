Job Description:
Standard Chartered is hiring a Senior Information Security Risk Manager to lead enterprise-wide risk management programs aligned with global regulatory standards.

Responsibilities:
- Lead enterprise risk assessments aligned with ISO 27001 and NIST CSF
- Own the risk register and drive remediation tracking across business units
- Oversee third-party risk management program using OneTrust
- Manage SOC 2 Type II audit lifecycle from preparation to certification
- Provide executive-level reporting on risk posture and GDPR compliance
- Lead a team of 3 junior analysts

Requirements:
- 8+ years experience in information security risk management
- CISSP certification required
- CISA or CRISC certification strongly preferred
- Deep knowledge of ISO 27001, NIST CSF, SOC 2, GDPR, and MAS TRM guidelines
- Hands-on experience with ServiceNow GRC and Archer
- Experience managing regulatory examinations
- Strong stakeholder management and executive communication skills
