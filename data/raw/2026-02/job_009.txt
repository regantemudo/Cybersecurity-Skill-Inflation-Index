Title: GRC Analyst - Entry Level
Company: Deloitte Advisory
Location: London, United Kingdom
Salary: GBP 35,000 - 42,000 annually

Job Description:
Deloitte Advisory is seeking an Entry Level GRC Analyst to join our growing cybersecurity consulting practice. You will support client engagements across risk, governance, and compliance.

Responsibilities:
- Support ISO 27001 gap assessments and implementation projects
- Assist with NIST CSF maturity assessments for financial sector clients
- Maintain client risk registers and prepare compliance status reports
- Contribute to SOC 2 readiness engagements
- Document policies, procedures, and control frameworks

Requirements:
- 1+ years experience in information security or IT audit
- Understanding of ISO 27001, NIST CSF, and SOC 2
- CompTIA Security+ or CEH certification preferred
- Strong documentation and analytical skills
- Willingness to travel to client sites when required
- CISA certification a strong advantage
