Title: Cybersecurity Compliance Manager
Company: Emirates NBD
Location: Dubai, UAE
Salary: AED 22,000 - 28,000 per month

Job Description:
Emirates NBD is seeking a Cybersecurity Compliance Manager to oversee all compliance activities for the bank's information security program in line with UAE CBUAE regulations and international standards.

Responsibilities:
- Manage ISO 27001 certification program and annual surveillance audits
- Ensure compliance with GDPR, NIST, PCI-DSS, and CBUAE cybersecurity regulations
- Lead third-party vendor risk assessments using Archer
- Oversee security awareness and training program
- Report compliance posture to CISO and board-level committees
- Coordinate with external auditors and regulators

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in cybersecurity compliance or information security management
- CISA certification required
- CISSP or ISO 27001 Lead Auditor certification preferred
- Strong knowledge of PCI-DSS, GDPR, NIST CSF, and UAE banking regulations
- Experience with Archer or ServiceNow GRC modules
- Proven team leadership experience
