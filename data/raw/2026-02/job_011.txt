Title: Information Security Analyst - Mid Level
Company: Infosys Cyber Defense
Location: Hyderabad, India
Salary: INR 12,00,000 - 16,00,000 annually

Job Description:
Infosys Cyber Defense is hiring a mid-level Information Security Analyst to support client security operations, risk management, and compliance programs.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and risk analysis for enterprise clients
- Support ISO 27001 internal audits and NIST CSF assessments
- Monitor Splunk SIEM dashboards and triage security incidents
- Maintain SOC 2 control documentation and evidence collection
- Perform GDPR data mapping and privacy impact assessments
- Prepare executive-level compliance reports

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in information security or IT risk
- ISO 27001 Lead Implementer or CISA certification preferred
- Hands-on experience with Splunk and ServiceNow
- Knowledge of NIST CSF, SOC 2, and GDPR frameworks
- Strong scripting skills (Python or PowerShell) for automation
- CEH or CompTIA CySA+ is a plus
