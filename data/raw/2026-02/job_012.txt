FinSecure Asia is seeking a strategic CISO to lead the enterprise cybersecurity function across Asia-Pacific operations, driving risk strategy, regulatory compliance, and security culture.

- Define and execute enterprise cybersecurity strategy across APAC
- Own ISO 27001, SOC 2, MAS TRM, and HKMA cybersecurity compliance programs
- Lead enterprise risk management framework using ServiceNow and Archer
- Oversee GDPR and PDPO data privacy compliance
- Manage relationships with regulators, auditors, and board-level stakeholders
- Lead a team of 12 security professionals across 4 countries
- Drive security culture through executive-level awareness programs

Requirements:
- 12+ years experience in information security leadership
- CISSP certification required
- CISA and CRISC certifications required
- Deep expertise in ISO 27001, NIST CSF, SOC 2, GDPR, MAS TRM, and HKMA guidelines
- Proven experience with ServiceNow GRC, Archer, and OneTrust
- Track record of leading regulatory examinations and board-level reporting
- MBA or equivalent senior leadership qualification preferred
