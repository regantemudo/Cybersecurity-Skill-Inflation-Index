Title: SOC Analyst Level 2
Company: ThreatGuard Security
Location: Dubai, UAE
Salary: AED 16,000 - 20,000 per month

Job Description:
ThreatGuard is hiring a SOC Analyst L2 to monitor, detect, and respond to cybersecurity incidents across enterprise clients using a 24x7 security operations center.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor and triage alerts from Splunk SIEM and Microsoft Sentinel
- Investigate security incidents using CrowdStrike Falcon EDR
- Perform threat hunting and IOC analysis
- Develop and tune detection rules and playbooks
- Escalate P1/P2 incidents and lead initial response
- Produce shift handover and incident reports

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in SOC or incident response
- Hands-on experience with Splunk and Microsoft Sentinel
- Familiarity with MITRE ATT&CK framework
- CrowdStrike Falcon proficiency required
- CompTIA CySA+ or GIAC GCIH certification preferred
- Security+ certification required
- Understanding of NIST incident response lifecycle
