Title: Threat Intelligence Analyst
Company: CyberEdge Asia
Location: Singapore
Salary: SGD 7,000 - 9,000 per month

Job Description:
CyberEdge Asia is seeking a Threat Intelligence Analyst to deliver actionable threat intelligence for financial sector clients across APAC.

Responsibilities:
- Collect and analyse threat intelligence from OSINT, dark web, and commercial feeds
- Develop threat profiles and TTPs using MITRE ATT&CK framework
- Produce finished intelligence products for executive and technical audiences
- Integrate threat intel into SIEM and SOAR platforms
- Support incident response with adversary context
- Track APT groups targeting the financial sector

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in cyber threat intelligence or SOC
- Strong knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK and Diamond Model
- Experience with Splunk and CrowdStrike or similar EDR
- GIAC GCTI or equivalent certification preferred
- CISSP or Security+ certification required
- Scripting skills (Python) for automation
- Familiarity with STIX/TAXII and threat intel platforms
