Title: Application Security Engineer
Company: SecureCode Labs
Location: Hong Kong
Salary: HKD 45,000 - 60,000 per month

Job Description:
SecureCode Labs is hiring an Application Security Engineer to embed security into the SDLC for a leading digital banking platform.

Responsibilities:
- Perform secure code reviews and threat modeling for new features
- Integrate SAST tools (Checkmarx, Semgrep) and DAST (Burp Suite) into CI/CD pipelines
- Conduct web application penetration testing aligned with OWASP Top 10
- Lead security champions program across engineering teams
- Define secure coding standards and developer training
- Perform SCA using Black Duck and Snyk
- Manage vulnerability triage and remediation SLAs

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in application security
- Strong knowledge of OWASP Top 10 and OWASP SAMM
- Experience with Checkmarx, Semgrep, Burp Suite, Snyk
- CSSLP or GWEB certification preferred
- Understanding of GDPR and PCI-DSS requirements for application security
- Proficiency in at least one programming language (Java, Python, or JavaScript)
- OSWE or GWAPT certification a strong advantage
