Title: Cybersecurity Governance Manager
Company: ANZ Banking Group
Location: Melbourne, VIC, Australia
Salary: AUD 130,000 - 160,000 annually
Domain: GRC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-02-20

Job Description:
ANZ Banking Group is seeking a Cybersecurity Governance Manager to lead the bank's information security governance framework, ensuring alignment with APRA CPS 234, ISO 27001, and NIST CSF across Australian and New Zealand operations.

Responsibilities:
- Own and maintain ANZ's Information Security Policy framework including 40+ security standards and procedures
- Lead the bank's APRA CPS 234 compliance program including annual attestation to the Board
- Manage the cybersecurity risk register and report risk posture to the CISO and Board Risk Committee
- Conduct annual ISO 27001 surveillance audits and manage certification renewal
- Oversee third-party and supplier cybersecurity risk assessments across 200+ vendors
- Deliver quarterly cybersecurity governance reporting to executive and board audiences
- Lead a team of 3 governance analysts and manage stakeholder relationships across 12 business units

Requirements:
- 7+ years experience in cybersecurity governance, risk, or compliance
- ISO 27001 Lead Auditor certification required
- CISM or CISSP required
- Deep knowledge of APRA CPS 234 and APRA CPG 234 guidelines
- CRISC certification preferred
- Experience with GRC platforms: Archer or ServiceNow GRC
- Strong executive communication and board reporting skills
- Familiarity with NIST CSF and ASD Essential Eight framework
