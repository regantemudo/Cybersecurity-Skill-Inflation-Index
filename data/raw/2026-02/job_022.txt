Title: Information Security Risk Analyst

Salary: INR 10,00,000 - 15,00,000 annually

Job Description:
Wipro GRC Practice is seeking an Information Security Risk Analyst to support enterprise risk management and compliance engagements for banking and financial services clients across the APAC region.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct information security risk assessments using ISO 27005 and NIST RMF methodologies
- Perform vendor and third-party risk assessments against defined security control frameworks
- Develop and maintain risk registers, risk treatment plans, and control evidence repositories
- Support ISO 27001 ISMS implementation and internal audit activities
- Assist in regulatory compliance gap analysis for RBI, MAS, and APRA requirements
- Prepare risk dashboards and monthly status reports for client CISOs
- Coordinate with IT and business teams on control implementation and remediation tracking

Requirements:
- 2-4 years experience in information security risk management or IT audit
- ISO 27001 Lead Implementer certification required
- CISA or CRISC certification preferred
- Knowledge of NIST RMF, ISO 27005, and RBI IT Framework
- Experience with GRC tools: Archer, MetricStream, or ServiceNow GRC
- Strong documentation and analytical skills
- Understanding of financial sector regulatory requirements across APAC
