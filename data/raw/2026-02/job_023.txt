Job Description:
Protera Technologies is seeking a SOC Analyst for a 24x7 rotational remote role focused on continuous monitoring, detection, and incident response using advanced security technologies.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor and analyze security alerts from SIEM, EDR, NDR, FIM, and Antivirus/NGAV platforms
- Perform threat correlation and pattern analysis across multiple data sources
- Execute full incident response lifecycle: triage, containment, eradication, and recovery per NIST standards
- Develop and maintain incident response playbooks and SOAR automation workflows
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and coordinate remediation with IT teams
- Perform threat hunting based on known TTPs using MITRE ATT&CK and Cyber Kill Chain
- Generate dashboards and reports on MTTD, MTTR, and SOC performance metrics
- Ensure compliance with ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, and NIST CSF

Requirements:
- 4+ years of experience in SOC operations, cybersecurity, or incident response
- Strong knowledge of EDR, SIEM, SOAR, NDR, FIM, and Antivirus/NGAV platforms
- Experience writing playbooks, automating responses, and tuning detection logic
- SIEM tools: Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, QRadar, Elastic
- EDR/NDR: CrowdStrike Falcon, Microsoft Defender, SentinelOne, Darktrace
- SOAR: Cortex XSOAR, Splunk SOAR, Microsoft Sentinel Automation
- Certifications preferred: CompTIA Security+, CySA+, CEH, Microsoft SC-200
- Familiarity with MITRE ATT&CK, Cyber Kill Chain, NIST IR frameworks
