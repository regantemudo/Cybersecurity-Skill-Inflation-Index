Job Description:
Banyan Cloud, a U.S.-based cybersecurity product company headquartered in San Jose, California, is seeking an Associate Cyber Security Analyst to join their security team in Bangalore. The role focuses on vulnerability assessment and penetration testing across cloud-native environments.

- Perform penetration testing on web applications, networks, and systems
- Utilize ethical hacking techniques to simulate real-world cyber attacks
- Document findings and produce comprehensive reports for technical and non-technical audiences
- Stay current with cybersecurity trends, vulnerabilities, and best practices
- Collaborate with senior team members on real-world security scenarios

Requirements:
- 1-2 years of experience in cybersecurity or penetration testing (freshers with strong knowledge also considered)
- Familiarity with penetration testing tools: Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nmap
- Strong understanding of public clouds: AWS, GCP, Azure
- Knowledge of vulnerability assessment methodologies
- Familiarity with NIST and ISO 27001 frameworks
- Bug bounty experience is a plus
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field
