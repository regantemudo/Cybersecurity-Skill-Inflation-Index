Job Description:
Tech Vistar IT Solutions is hiring a Cyber Security Specialist to support day-to-day security operations including incident response, proactive threat hunting, endpoint security monitoring, and vulnerability management across 2500+ endpoints and cloud environments (AWS and Azure).

Responsibilities:
- Triage, analyse, and respond to security alerts (P1-P4) escalated by the SOC
- Perform full incident lifecycle: investigation, containment, mitigation, and recovery
- Conduct proactive threat hunting using EDR/XDR telemetry and threat intelligence feeds
- Build hypothesis-driven hunting queries to identify lateral movement, privilege misuse, and persistence
- Manage Trend Micro XDR across 2500+ endpoints and servers
- Monitor and respond to phishing, spoofing, and malware via email security gateway
- Perform vulnerability scanning and patch management using SecPod
- Coordinate with outsourced SOC/SOAR partner and internal IT teams
- Maintain dashboards for Incident Response, Threat Hunting, and Vulnerability KPIs

Requirements:
- 4-5 years experience in security operations, EDR/XDR, vulnerability management, or SOC
- CEH and CompTIA CySA+ certification required
- Practical knowledge of incident response methodologies and threat hunting (IOC/IOA analysis)
- Experience with Trend Micro XDR or similar EDR/XDR tools
- Basic AWS/Azure security concepts
- Strong documentation and analytical skills
