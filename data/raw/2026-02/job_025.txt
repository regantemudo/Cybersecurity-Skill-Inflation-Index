Job Description: Cyber Security Specialist (Incident Response, Threat Hunting & Vulnerability Management)

Reporting to: Manager – Cyber Security

Department: Information Security

Location: [Mumbai, Goregaon West]-On-Site

Role Type: Full-Time

1. Role Overview

The Cyber Security Specialist will support day-to-day security operations by managing incident response, performing proactive

threat hunting, monitoring endpoint and email security, and driving vulnerability and patch management activities across the

organisation. The role involves close coordination with the outsourced SOC/SOAR partner and internal IT teams to maintain a

strong security posture across 2500+ endpoints, servers, and cloud environments (AWS & Azure).

2. Key Responsibilities

A. Security Incident Response

    Triage, analyse, and respond to security alerts (P1–P4) escalated by the SOC.
    Perform full incident lifecycle activities: investigation, containment, mitigation, and recovery.
    Validate alerts, eliminate false positives, and correlate telemetry across multiple tools.
    Maintain incident logs, RCA documents, evidence, and audit compliance artefacts.
    Escalate critical incidents as per defined SLAs.

B. Threat Hunting (Proactive Security Defence)

    Conduct proactive threat hunting across endpoints, servers, email, and logs to detect early-stage or hidden threats.
    Use Trend Micro XDR telemetry, EDR behavioural indicators, IOC/IOA patterns, and threat intel feeds to identify

suspicious activity.

    Build and execute hypothesis-driven hunting queries to identify anomalies (e.g., lateral movement, privilege misuse,

persistence mechanisms, suspicious PowerShell activity).

    Develop and refine detection rules, alert logic, and hunting playbooks.
    Perform retrospective analysis on historical logs to uncover missed detections.
    Document hunting findings and recommend security control improvements.

C. Endpoint Security (Trend Micro XDR)

    Investigate and remediate detections from 2500+ endpoints and server nodes.
    Ensure agent health, policy enforcement, and up-to-date signatures.
    Assist in system isolation and coordinated remediation actions with IT Infra teams.
    Prepare weekly and monthly endpoint threat analytics and reports.

D. Email Security Gateway

    Monitor and respond to phishing, spoofing, malware, and URL-based threats.
    Review quarantined messages, manage blocklists/allowlists, and investigate suspicious email campaigns.
    Analyse email logs and generate periodic summaries for the security team.

E. Vulnerability & Patch Management (SecPod)

    Perform continuous vulnerability scanning and patching cycles for endpoints and servers.
    Validate scan results, remove false positives, and coordinate remediation with relevant teams.
    Track patch compliance, non-compliant assets, and SLA deviations.
    Maintain dashboards and documentation for audit closure.

F. Coordination with SOC/SOAR & OEM Partners

    Serve as the internal escalation point for SOC alerts, investigations, and SLA tracking.
    Coordinate with Trend Micro, SecPod, and Email Gateway OEMs for technical support and issue resolution.
    Collaborate with IT Infra, Cloud, Network and Application teams for cross-functional remediation.

G. Security Governance & Reporting

    Maintain dashboards for Incident Response, Threat Hunting outcomes, Patch Compliance, and Vulnerability KPIs.
    Support external/internal security audits, compliance frameworks, and policy implementations.
    Prepare MIS reports for senior management.

3. Required Skills & Experience

    Preferred bachelor’s degree in computer science / IT / Cyber Security or Any Graduate.
    CEH Certificate, CompTIA CySA+ (Cybersecurity Analyst)
    4–5 years hands-on experience in security operations, EDR/XDR, vulnerability management, or SOC environments.
    Practical knowledge of:

o Incident Response methodologies

o Threat hunting techniques (IOC/IOA analysis, memory forensics basics, log pattern analysis)

o Trend Micro XDR or similar EDR/XDR tools

o Email Security Gateway tools

o Vulnerability management and patch compliance platforms (SecPod or equivalents)

o Basic AWS/Azure security concepts

    Strong analytical, documentation, and problem-solving skills.
    Ability to work under pressure and manage high alert volumes.
    Good communication and coordination skills.

4. Core Competencies

    High urgency mindset
    Analytical thinking and attention to detail
    Proactive security approach (not just alert-driven)
    Strong ownership
    Ability to handle 500+ alerts/month environment
