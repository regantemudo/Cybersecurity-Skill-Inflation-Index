Job Title: Security Analyst

Location: Kochi

Major Objectives of the Job:

    Execute Security Testing Services delivery

o Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing ( systems, networks, web-based and mobile)

o Secure Code Review (SAST, DAST)

o Secure Config Review (Cloud, On-Premise)

    Review technical findings, test cases, test plan and security testing reports

    Timeliness of Report Delivery, ensures timely distribution of reports

    Resolve client issues and escalations

    Provide recommendations/solution/remediation for security improvements to Customers

Main Responsibilities:

1. Quality of Service Delivery

    Accuracy of Project Scope and Deliverables

    Quality of Security Testing Process

    Quality of Report Delivery

2. Customer relation

    Communication skills (oral, written)

    On-time delivery

    Client Issue Resolution Rate

3. Security Testing Process Efficiency

    Developing/following process

    Develop automation frameworks to enhance efficiency

    Using tools/scripts efficiently

4. Continuous Improvement & Innovation

    Implement innovative ideas and approaches to security testing

    Learning new tools, attacks, vulnerabilities, exploits

Competencies Required:

    Exp VA/PT – 1 – 3 years

Skills:

    Scripting & Programming skills Java, php, python, Javascript or Net technologies

    Database concepts and query execution

    Good understanding of network protocols

Certifications:

    CEH, eJPT, CRTP, OSCP or similar.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Pay: Up to ₹700,000.00 per year

Benefits:

    Health insurance
    Provident Fund

Application Question(s):

    How many years of experience you have in VAPT ?
    Do you have any experience in Systems, Networks, Web-based and Mobile based testing ?

    What about your experience in Secure Code Review (SAST, DAST) and Secure Config Review (Cloud, On-Premise) ?
    Are you an Immediate Joiner ?

Experience:

    minimum: 3 years (Required)

Work Location: In person
