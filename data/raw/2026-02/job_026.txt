Requirements:
- 1-3 years experience in VA/PT (minimum 3 years required)
- Scripting and programming skills: Java, PHP, Python, JavaScript or .NET
- Good understanding of network protocols and database concepts
- Certifications: CEH, eJPT, CRTP, or OSCP preferred
- Experience in systems, networks, web-based, and mobile-based testing
- Knowledge of SAST, DAST, and secure config review
