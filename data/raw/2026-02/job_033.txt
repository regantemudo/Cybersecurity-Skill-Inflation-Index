Job Description:
American Express Global Business Travel (Amex GBT) is seeking an experienced IAM Engineer to implement and manage Identity Governance and Administration using Saviynt, supporting identity lifecycle management, access controls, and compliance across the enterprise.

Responsibilities:
- Design, implement, and manage Saviynt IAM solutions for access controls and identity lifecycle management
- Handle user provisioning, de-provisioning, and account modifications
- Design and maintain identity management solutions for non-human identities including service accounts and applications
- Configure and deploy Saviynt modules and connectors for various platforms
- Identify and assess IAM-related security risks and implement mitigation strategies
- Establish monitoring and reporting mechanisms for audit and compliance purposes
- Investigate and respond to IAM-related security incidents
- Execute reports and produce IAM metrics including KPIs

Requirements:
- Minimum 5 years hands-on experience in IAM
- Minimum 3 years hands-on experience with Saviynt including security system, entitlements, and workflows
- Experience with IGA platforms: SailPoint IdentityNow, IdentityIQ, or OIM as alternative
- Hands-on experience with REST and SOAP connectors, JSON building and mapping
- Proficiency with Postman, JavaScript, and PowerShell scripting
- Knowledge of SSO, MFA, and Privileged Access Management (PAM)
- Experience with CyberArk, Azure AD, AWS, Active Directory, and Okta preferred
- Familiarity with SOC 2, ISO, SOX, and PCI compliance requirements
- CISSP or security certification a plus
