Title: Threat Intelligence Analyst
Company: Mandiant (Google Cloud)
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: Not Listed
Domain: SOC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-02-24

Job Description:
Mandiant, part of Google Cloud, is seeking a Threat Intelligence Analyst to track advanced persistent threat (APT) groups and produce finished intelligence products for enterprise and government clients across the APAC region.

Responsibilities:
- Track and profile APT groups targeting financial, government, and critical infrastructure sectors
- Produce tactical, operational, and strategic threat intelligence reports
- Develop threat actor profiles using MITRE ATT&CK TTP mappings
- Monitor dark web forums, paste sites, and underground markets for threat signals
- Integrate threat intelligence into SIEM and SOAR platforms
- Brief executive and technical audiences on emerging threat landscape
- Collaborate with incident response teams to provide adversary context during active investigations

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in cyber threat intelligence or security operations
- Strong knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK, Diamond Model, and Kill Chain frameworks
- Experience with threat intelligence platforms: Recorded Future, MISP, or OpenCTI
- Proficiency with Splunk or Microsoft Sentinel
- GIAC GCTI or equivalent certification preferred
- CISSP or Security+ certification required
- Python scripting for automation and data enrichment
- Familiarity with STIX/TAXII protocols
