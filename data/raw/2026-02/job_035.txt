Location: Singapore

Job Description:
CrowdStrike is seeking a Senior SOC Analyst at the L3 level to lead complex threat investigations and mentor junior analysts across a global 24x7 security operations function focused on OverWatch managed threat hunting.

Responsibilities:
- Lead complex incident investigations using CrowdStrike Falcon platform
- Perform advanced threat hunting across enterprise customer environments
- Develop custom detection logic and correlation rules in Splunk and Falcon
- Mentor L1 and L2 analysts and review their triage outputs
- Produce detailed adversary simulation reports with MITRE ATT&CK mappings
- Respond to P1 critical incidents and lead client communication
- Build and maintain SOAR playbooks for automated response workflows

Requirements:
- 6+ years experience in SOC, incident response, or threat hunting
- Expert-level CrowdStrike Falcon proficiency required
- Strong knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK, Cyber Kill Chain, and NIST IR
- Experience with Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, or QRadar
- GCIH or GCFE certification required
- CISSP certification strongly preferred
- Microsoft SC-200 or CrowdStrike CCFA certification a plus
- Scripting in Python or PowerShell for automation
