Title: Mobile Application Penetration Tester
Company: NCC Group
Location: London, United Kingdom
Salary: GBP 60,000 - 78,000 annually
Domain: Penetration Testing
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-02-24

Job Description:
NCC Group is seeking a Mobile Application Penetration Tester to conduct in-depth security assessments of iOS and Android applications for clients across financial services, healthcare, and government sectors.

Responsibilities:
- Perform iOS and Android mobile application penetration testing
- Conduct static and dynamic analysis of mobile applications
- Test for OWASP Mobile Top 10 vulnerabilities
- Assess backend API security alongside mobile app testing
- Write detailed technical and executive-level penetration test reports
- Assist in developing new mobile testing methodologies and tooling
- Present findings to technical and non-technical client stakeholders

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in mobile or web application penetration testing
- OSCP required; GMOB or eMAPT certification strongly preferred
- Proficiency with tools: Frida, Objection, MobSF, Jadx, Burp Suite
- Strong knowledge of OWASP Mobile Top 10 and PTES methodology
- Experience with iOS jailbreaking and Android rooting for testing purposes
- Scripting skills in Python or JavaScript
- CEH or GWAPT certification a plus
