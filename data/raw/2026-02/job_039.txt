Title: Product Security Engineer
Company: Razorpay
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: INR 30,00,000 - 45,00,000 annually
Domain: AppSec
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-02-24

Job Description:
Razorpay is seeking a Product Security Engineer to build and scale application security programs across its payment infrastructure, ensuring secure-by-design principles are embedded throughout the SDLC.

Responsibilities:
- Perform security design reviews and threat modelling for new product features
- Conduct manual and automated code reviews across Java, Go, and Node.js codebases
- Integrate and manage SAST tools (Semgrep, Checkmarx) and DAST (Burp Suite) in CI/CD
- Build and maintain SCA pipeline using Snyk for open source vulnerability management
- Develop and deliver developer security training and secure coding guidelines
- Run bug bounty program triage and coordinate researcher communications
- Define and track vulnerability remediation SLAs across engineering teams

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in application security or product security
- Strong knowledge of OWASP Top 10, OWASP SAMM, and threat modelling (STRIDE)
- Experience with Semgrep, Checkmarx, Burp Suite, and Snyk
- Proficiency in at least one programming language: Java, Go, Python, or Node.js
- CSSLP or GWEB certification preferred
- PCI-DSS knowledge required (payment industry)
- OSWE or GWAPT certification a strong advantage
