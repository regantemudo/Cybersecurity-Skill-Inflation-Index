Title: Privileged Access Management Engineer
Company: Standard Chartered Bank
Location: Singapore
Salary: SGD 9,500 - 13,000 per month
Domain: IAM

Job Description:
Standard Chartered Bank is seeking a Privileged Access Management Engineer to manage and mature the bank's PAM program using CyberArk across global infrastructure in 50+ countries.

Responsibilities:
- Implement and manage CyberArk Privileged Access Security (PAS) suite including Vault, PVWA, CPM, and PSM
- Onboard privileged accounts across Windows, Linux, network devices, and cloud platforms
- Define and enforce least-privilege access policies and just-in-time provisioning
- Integrate CyberArk with LDAP, Active Directory, and Azure AD
- Conduct PAM health checks and access certification campaigns
- Support regulatory compliance audits for MAS TRM, SOC 2, and ISO 27001
- Troubleshoot and resolve PAM platform incidents and service requests

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in PAM or identity security
- CyberArk Defender or Sentry certification required
- Hands-on experience with CyberArk PAS suite, EPM, and Alero
- Knowledge of LDAP, Active Directory, Azure AD, and SAML federation
- CISSP or CISM certification strongly preferred
- Understanding of MAS TRM and financial sector regulatory requirements
- Scripting skills in PowerShell or Python for automation
