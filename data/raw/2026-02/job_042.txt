Title: Identity Governance Engineer

Location: Hyderabad, India
Salary: INR 18,00,000 - 26,00,000 annually

Job Description:
Infosys is seeking an Identity Governance Engineer to implement and manage SailPoint IdentityNow for a large financial services client, enabling automated joiner/mover/leaver processes and access certification campaigns.

Responsibilities:
- Implement and configure SailPoint IdentityNow including sources, roles, and policies
- Develop and maintain provisioning workflows and access request processes
- Build and manage access certification campaigns for SOX and ISO 27001 compliance
- Integrate IdentityNow with HR systems (Workday, SAP HCM) for lifecycle automation
- Design role-based access control (RBAC) and segregation of duties (SoD) policies
- Perform identity analytics and build dashboards for access governance reporting
- Troubleshoot provisioning failures and maintain SailPoint connectors

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience with SailPoint IdentityNow or IdentityIQ
- SailPoint Certified IdentityNow Engineer preferred
- Experience integrating IGA with Active Directory, Azure AD, and cloud applications
- Knowledge of SAML, OAuth 2.0, SCIM, and OIDC protocols
- CISSP, CISM, or SC-300 certification preferred
- Strong scripting in BeanShell, Java, or PowerShell
- Understanding of SOX, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance requirements
- Experience with Workday or SAP HCM integration
