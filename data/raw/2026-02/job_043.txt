Title: Cloud Identity Security Architect
Company: Deloitte Cyber Risk
Location: London, United Kingdom
Salary: GBP 90,000 - 120,000 annually
Domain: IAM
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-02-24

Job Description:
Deloitte Cyber Risk is seeking a Cloud Identity Security Architect to lead Zero Trust identity transformation programs for global enterprise clients, designing next-generation IAM architectures across hybrid and multi-cloud environments.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement Zero Trust identity architectures across Azure AD, AWS IAM, and GCP IAM
- Lead Entra ID (Azure AD) deployments including Conditional Access, PIM, and Privileged Identity Management
- Architect SSO and MFA strategies across 200+ enterprise applications
- Define identity security roadmaps aligned with NIST Zero Trust Architecture framework
- Conduct IAM maturity assessments and produce executive-level recommendations
- Manage delivery teams of junior architects and consultants
- Engage with C-suite stakeholders on identity risk and strategy

Requirements:
- 8+ years experience in IAM with at least 3 years in cloud identity architecture
- Microsoft SC-300 and AZ-500 certifications required
- Expert knowledge of Azure AD, Entra ID, AWS IAM, and GCP IAM
- Deep understanding of SAML, OAuth 2.0, OIDC, and SCIM protocols
- Experience with Okta, Ping Identity, or SailPoint at enterprise scale
- CISSP required; CCSP preferred
- Knowledge of NIST Zero Trust, ISO 27001, and GDPR
- Strong executive communication and consulting skills
