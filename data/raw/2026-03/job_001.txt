Title: SOC Analyst - Tier 2
Company: Secureworks
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: INR 14,00,000 - 20,00,000 annually
Domain: SOC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-01

Job Description:
Secureworks is seeking a Tier 2 SOC Analyst to investigate escalated alerts, perform in-depth threat analysis, and support incident response operations for enterprise clients across the APAC region.

Responsibilities:
- Investigate and triage security alerts escalated from Tier 1 analysts
- Perform deep-dive analysis using SIEM, EDR, and network telemetry
- Develop and tune detection rules in Splunk and Microsoft Sentinel
- Conduct threat hunting using MITRE ATT&CK TTPs
- Produce detailed incident reports and post-incident reviews
- Mentor Tier 1 analysts and review their investigation outputs
- Coordinate with client security teams during active incidents

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in SOC, incident response, or threat analysis
- Strong hands-on knowledge of Splunk and Microsoft Sentinel
- Experience with CrowdStrike Falcon or SentinelOne EDR platforms
- Familiarity with MITRE ATT&CK, Cyber Kill Chain, and NIST IR
- CompTIA CySA+ or Microsoft SC-200 required
- CEH or GCIA certification preferred
- Python or PowerShell scripting for log analysis and automation
