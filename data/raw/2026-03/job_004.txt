Title: Bug Bounty Hunter - Security Researcher
Company: HackerOne
Location: Remote - Global
Salary: USD 80,000 - 120,000 annually
Domain: Penetration Testing
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-01

Job Description:
HackerOne is seeking a Security Researcher to join their internal red team, triaging and validating vulnerability reports submitted through their platform while also conducting internal security assessments for the HackerOne product.

Responsibilities:
- Triage and validate vulnerability reports across web, API, and mobile attack surfaces
- Reproduce reported vulnerabilities and assess severity using CVSS scoring
- Conduct internal penetration tests on HackerOne's own infrastructure
- Develop proof-of-concept exploits to validate report impact
- Communicate technical findings to both security and non-technical audiences
- Build automation to improve triage speed and consistency
- Contribute to public security research and CVE disclosures

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in penetration testing or bug bounty research
- Strong knowledge of OWASP Top 10 and OWASP API Security Top 10
- Proficiency with Burp Suite Pro, ffuf, Nuclei, and custom tooling
- Experience with web, API, and mobile application security testing
- OSCP or OSWE preferred
- Active bug bounty track record on HackerOne or Bugcrowd a strong advantage
- Scripting in Python or JavaScript for automation
