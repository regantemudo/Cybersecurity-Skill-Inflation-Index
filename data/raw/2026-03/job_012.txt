Job Description:
DXC Technology is seeking a Security Operations Engineer specialising in SIEM administration and detection engineering to support a global financial services client's security operations across the Asia Pacific region.

Responsibilities:
- Administer, tune, and optimise Microsoft Sentinel and Splunk SIEM platforms
- Develop and maintain detection rules, analytics, and watchlists
- Build KQL queries and Splunk SPL searches for threat detection use cases
- Manage SOAR playbook development in Sentinel Automation and Cortex XSOAR
- Onboard new data sources and ensure log ingestion quality
- Perform use case lifecycle management from requirements to deployment
- Produce weekly detection effectiveness reports for client stakeholders

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in SOC engineering or SIEM administration
- Microsoft Sentinel and Splunk hands-on experience required
- Microsoft SC-200 certification required
- Proficiency in KQL and Splunk SPL for detection logic
- Experience with SOAR platforms: Sentinel Automation or Cortex XSOAR
- CompTIA CySA+ or GCIA certification preferred
- Python scripting for log parsing and automation
- Knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK for detection mapping
