Title: Identity Security Analyst - CyberArk
Company: HDFC Bank
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Salary: INR 15,00,000 - 22,00,000 annually
Domain: IAM
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-10

Job Description:
HDFC Bank is seeking an Identity Security Analyst with CyberArk expertise to manage privileged access governance across one of India's largest private banks, supporting 80,000+ employees and 5,000+ privileged accounts across hybrid infrastructure.

Responsibilities:
- Manage CyberArk PAS suite including Vault, PVWA, CPM, PSM, and PSMP
- Onboard privileged accounts for Windows servers, Linux, network devices, databases, and cloud platforms
- Conduct quarterly privileged access certification campaigns and generate audit reports
- Implement just-in-time (JIT) access provisioning workflows
- Support RBI IT Framework, ISO 27001, and internal audit requirements
- Prepare monthly PAM metrics dashboards for CISO reporting
- Troubleshoot CyberArk platform incidents and coordinate with vendor support

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in PAM administration or identity security
- CyberArk Defender certification required
- CyberArk Sentry or Guardian preferred
- Working knowledge of Active Directory, Windows Server, and Linux
- CISSP or SC-300 certification preferred
- PowerShell scripting for CyberArk automation and reporting
- Understanding of RBI IT Framework and SEBI cybersecurity guidelines
- Familiarity with ISO 27001 audit requirements
