Title: Cybersecurity Risk Analyst
Company: Deloitte Risk Advisory
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Salary: INR 12,00,000 - 18,00,000 annually

Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-10

Job Description:
Deloitte Risk Advisory is seeking a Cybersecurity Risk Analyst to support GRC engagements for large financial services and manufacturing clients, delivering risk assessments, control framework implementations, and regulatory compliance programs.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct cybersecurity risk assessments aligned with ISO 27001, NIST CSF, and RBI guidelines
- Perform gap assessments against regulatory frameworks including SEBI, IRDAI, and RBI IT Framework
- Develop and maintain information security policies, standards, and procedures
- Support third-party vendor risk assessments and supply chain security reviews
- Assist in internal audit readiness and evidence collection for ISO 27001 certification
- Prepare risk dashboards and board-level reporting for client CISOs
- Coordinate with IT and business teams during control implementation

Requirements:
- 2-4 years experience in cybersecurity GRC, risk management, or IT audit
- ISO 27001 Lead Implementer or Lead Auditor certification required
- Familiarity with NIST CSF, CIS Controls, and RBI IT Framework
- CISA or CRISC certification preferred
- Knowledge of SEBI Cybersecurity and Cyber Resilience Framework
- Strong documentation and report writing skills
- Proficiency with GRC tools: Archer, MetricStream, or ServiceNow GRC
