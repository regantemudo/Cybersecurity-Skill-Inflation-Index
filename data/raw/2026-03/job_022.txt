Title: Information Security Compliance Manager
Company: EY (Ernst & Young)

Salary: INR 18,00,000 - 28,00,000 annually

Job Description:
EY is seeking an Information Security Compliance Manager to lead SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and PCI-DSS compliance programs for technology and fintech clients across India and the Asia Pacific region.

Responsibilities:
- Lead end-to-end SOC 2 Type II readiness assessments and audit support engagements
- Manage ISO 27001 ISMS implementation and certification programs for clients
- Conduct PCI-DSS scoping, gap assessments, and remediation tracking
- Develop control matrices, risk registers, and evidence repositories
- Liaise with external auditors and manage audit timelines and deliverables
- Train client teams on compliance requirements and control ownership
- Deliver monthly compliance status reports to client executive teams

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in information security compliance or IT audit
- CISA certification required
- ISO 27001 Lead Auditor certification required
- PCI-DSS QSA qualification preferred
- Hands-on experience with SOC 2 audits and AICPA trust service criteria
- CISSP or CISM certification preferred
- Knowledge of GDPR, India DPDP Act, and cross-border data transfer requirements
- Strong stakeholder management and client communication skills
