Title: Senior IT Auditor - Cybersecurity
Company: PwC India
Location: Hyderabad, India
Salary: INR 15,00,000 - 22,00,000 annually
Domain: GRC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-10

Job Description:
PwC India is seeking a Senior IT Auditor with cybersecurity focus to conduct IT general controls reviews, cybersecurity audits, and SOX IT compliance assessments for large listed companies and financial institutions across India.

Responsibilities:
- Plan and execute IT general controls (ITGC) audits covering access management, change management, and operations
- Conduct cybersecurity maturity assessments aligned with NIST CSF and ISO 27001
- Perform SOX IT compliance testing for publicly listed clients and US cross-listed entities
- Assess cybersecurity controls in ERP environments including SAP and Oracle
- Identify control gaps and develop practical remediation recommendations
- Prepare audit reports and present findings to audit committees and senior management
- Supervise junior auditors and review their workpapers and deliverables

Requirements:
- 4-6 years experience in IT audit, cybersecurity audit, or risk advisory
- CISA certification required
- CRISC or CISSP preferred
- Knowledge of SOX IT compliance requirements and PCAOB standards
- Experience auditing SAP or Oracle ERP environments preferred
- ISO 27001 Lead Auditor certification a strong advantage
- Strong analytical, report writing, and presentation skills
- Familiarity with SEBI LODR and RBI regulatory audit requirements
