Title: GRC Analyst - Cloud Security Compliance

Job Description:
Cognizant Technology Solutions is seeking a GRC Analyst with cloud security compliance expertise to support a large US financial services client in maintaining SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and CSA STAR certifications across AWS and Azure cloud environments.

Responsibilities:
- Manage continuous compliance monitoring for SOC 2 Type II controls across cloud infrastructure
- Conduct evidence collection and control testing for ISO 27001 annual surveillance audits
- Perform cloud security configuration reviews against CIS Benchmarks for AWS and Azure
- Maintain the cloud risk register and track remediation of identified control gaps
- Liaise with external auditors during SOC 2 and ISO 27001 audit cycles
- Develop and maintain cloud security policies and standards aligned to CSA CCM
- Prepare monthly compliance dashboards for client CISO and leadership reporting

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in GRC, IT audit, or cloud security compliance
- ISO 27001 Lead Implementer or Lead Auditor certification required
- CISA certification required
- Knowledge of SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria and AICPA audit standards
- Familiarity with CSA Cloud Controls Matrix (CCM) and STAR certification
- Experience with AWS Security Hub or Azure Security Center for compliance monitoring
- CCSP or AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner preferred
- Strong analytical and documentation skills
