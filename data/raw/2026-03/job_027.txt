Job Description:
KPMG Cyber Response is seeking a DFIR Analyst specialising in malware analysis and threat intelligence to support incident response engagements and managed detection services for enterprise clients.

Responsibilities:
- Perform static and dynamic malware analysis using Ghidra, IDA Pro, and Any.run sandbox
- Conduct host-based forensics on Windows and Linux using Volatility and Autopsy
- Analyse memory dumps, disk images, and network captures for forensic artifacts
- Develop threat actor profiles and IOC sets using MITRE ATT&CK TTP mapping
- Support active incident response engagements including triage, containment, and remediation
- Produce forensic investigation reports suitable for legal proceedings

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in DFIR, malware analysis, or threat intelligence
- GCFA or GCFE certification required
- GREM (malware reverse engineering) preferred
- Proficiency with Volatility, Autopsy, Ghidra, and IDA Pro
- Experience with sandbox platforms: Any.run, Cuckoo, or Joe Sandbox
- Python scripting for forensic automation and IOC extraction
- Knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK, Diamond Model, and STIX/TAXII
