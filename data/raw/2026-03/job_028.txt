Title: Senior Incident Response Consultant
Company: Secureworks Incident Response
Location: Dubai, UAE
Salary: AED 30,000 - 45,000 per month
Domain: DFIR

Job Description:
Secureworks is seeking a Senior Incident Response Consultant to lead breach investigations, ransomware recoveries, and nation-state intrusion investigations for enterprise and government clients across the Middle East and Africa region.

Responsibilities:
- Lead end-to-end incident response engagements from initial triage through remediation
- Perform advanced host-based and network forensics across Windows, Linux, and cloud environments
- Investigate ransomware attacks including encryption analysis and data exfiltration assessment
- Conduct Active Directory and Azure AD compromise investigations
- Perform cloud forensics on AWS, Azure, and GCP during cloud-based incidents
- Deliver executive briefings and detailed technical investigation reports

Requirements:
- 6+ years experience in incident response or digital forensics
- GCFA and GCIH certifications required
- GCFE or GCFR preferred
- CISSP or CISM preferred
- Expert knowledge of Windows forensic artifacts and Active Directory attack paths
- Hands-on experience with Velociraptor, Axiom, and EnCase or FTK
- Cloud forensics experience on AWS, Azure, and GCP
