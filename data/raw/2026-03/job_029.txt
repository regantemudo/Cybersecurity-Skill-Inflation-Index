Title: OT/ICS Security Engineer
Company: Siemens Energy
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Salary: INR 20,00,000 - 32,00,000 annually
Domain: OT/ICS Security
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-15

Job Description:
Siemens Energy is seeking an OT/ICS Security Engineer to protect industrial control systems and SCADA infrastructure across power generation and transmission facilities in India.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct OT/ICS security assessments across SCADA and DCS environments at energy facilities
- Implement network segmentation between IT and OT networks using Purdue Model architecture
- Deploy and manage Claroty and Nozomi OT security monitoring platforms
- Perform vulnerability assessments of PLCs, HMIs, and SCADA systems
- Develop and maintain OT-specific incident response playbooks
- Support IEC 62443 compliance assessments and NERC CIP control implementations

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in OT/ICS security or industrial control systems
- GICSP (Global Industrial Cyber Security Professional) required
- IEC 62443 certification or training preferred
- Hands-on experience with Claroty, Nozomi, or Dragos OT security platforms
- Understanding of Modbus, DNP3, Profinet, and OPC-UA protocols
- CISSP or CISM preferred for senior candidates
- Electrical or electronics engineering background preferred
