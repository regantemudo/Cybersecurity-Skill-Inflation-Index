Title: ICS Cybersecurity Consultant
Company: Dragos Inc.
Location: Remote - Singapore
Salary: SGD 10,000 - 15,000 per month
Domain: OT/ICS Security
Source: Manual

Job Description:
Dragos is seeking an ICS Cybersecurity Consultant to deliver OT security assessments and architecture reviews for critical infrastructure clients across oil and gas, utilities, and manufacturing sectors in Asia Pacific.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct OT/ICS security architecture assessments using IEC 62443 and NERC CIP frameworks
- Perform passive network monitoring and asset inventory in live OT environments using Dragos Platform
- Execute OT-specific penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
- Lead ICS incident response engagements including forensics on industrial endpoints
- Develop OT security roadmaps and control implementation plans for client CISOs
- Deliver tabletop exercises simulating industrial cyber attacks

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in OT/ICS security or critical infrastructure protection
- GICSP certification required
- CSSA (Certified SCADA Security Architect) preferred
- Expert knowledge of IEC 62443, NERC CIP, and NIST SP 800-82
- Hands-on experience with Dragos Platform, Claroty, or Nozomi
- Deep understanding of industrial protocols: Modbus, DNP3, EtherNet/IP, OPC-UA
- OSCP preferred for penetration testing component
