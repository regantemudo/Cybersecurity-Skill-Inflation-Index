Title: Data Privacy Engineer
Company: Flipkart
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: INR 22,00,000 - 35,00,000 annually
Domain: Data Privacy
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-15

Job Description:
Flipkart is seeking a Data Privacy Engineer to build privacy-by-design capabilities into its e-commerce platform serving 400 million+ users, ensuring compliance with India's DPDP Act and GDPR.

Responsibilities:
- Lead Privacy Impact Assessments and DPIAs for new product features
- Implement data classification and tagging across Flipkart's data lake using Microsoft Purview and BigID
- Design and build consent management infrastructure aligned with DPDP Act requirements
- Deploy and manage DLP controls using Microsoft Purview DLP and Varonis
- Define data minimisation, retention, and deletion policies
- Collaborate with legal and product teams to embed privacy by design into SDLC

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in data privacy engineering or data governance
- CIPP/E or CIPP/US certification required
- CIPM certification preferred
- Hands-on experience with Microsoft Purview, BigID, or Varonis
- Deep knowledge of India DPDP Act 2023 and GDPR requirements
- Python or SQL scripting for data discovery and compliance automation
- Privacy by Design and Privacy by Default principles expertise
