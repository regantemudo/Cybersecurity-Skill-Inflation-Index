Title: Data Protection Officer (DPO)
Company: HDFC Life Insurance
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Salary: INR 28,00,000 - 45,00,000 annually
Domain: Data Privacy
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-03-15

Job Description:
HDFC Life Insurance is seeking a Data Protection Officer to lead the company's data privacy program covering 50 million+ policyholders, ensuring full compliance with India's DPDP Act and IRDAI guidelines.

Responsibilities:
- Serve as primary contact for the Data Protection Board of India under DPDP Act
- Lead enterprise data protection program including policies, procedures, and control framework
- Manage data breach notification process and coordinate with regulators within statutory timelines
- Conduct annual privacy audits and oversee third-party data processor assessments
- Review and approve Data Processing Agreements with vendors
- Deliver privacy awareness training across HDFC Life's 15,000+ workforce

Requirements:
- 7+ years experience in data privacy, legal, or compliance roles
- CIPP/E required; CIPP/US and CIPM preferred
- Law degree preferred with data privacy specialisation
- Deep expertise in India DPDP Act 2023, IRDAI cybersecurity framework, and GDPR
- Experience with OneTrust or TrustArc privacy management platforms
- CISSP or CISM certification preferred
