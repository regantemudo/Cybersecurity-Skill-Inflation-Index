Organization: Alignerr Position: Vulnerability Management Analyst Type: Contract / Task-Based Compensation: $35–$60 /hour Location: Remote Commitment: 10–40 hours/week

- 2+ years in vulnerability management, security operations, or infrastructure security
- Familiar with CVEs, scanners, patching workflows, and risk prioritization frameworks
