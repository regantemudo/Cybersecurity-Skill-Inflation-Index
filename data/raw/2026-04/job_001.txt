- Develop and tune SIEM correlation rules and detection use cases
- Produce incident reports and post-incident reviews for client stakeholders
- Coordinate with threat intelligence team to enrich IOCs and update watchlists

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in SOC operations or incident response
- Microsoft SC-200 certification required
- CompTIA CySA+ or GCIA preferred
- Hands-on experience with Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, and CrowdStrike Falcon
- Knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK, Cyber Kill Chain, and NIST IR frameworks
- Python scripting for alert automation and log parsing
