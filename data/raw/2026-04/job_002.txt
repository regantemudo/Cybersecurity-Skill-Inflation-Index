Title: Senior Threat Intelligence Analyst
Company: Group-IB
Location: Dubai, UAE
Salary: AED 25,000 - 38,000 per month
Domain: SOC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-05

Job Description:
Group-IB is seeking a Senior Threat Intelligence Analyst to produce finished threat intelligence products for financial sector clients across the Middle East, tracking cybercrime groups and APT actors targeting the region.

Responsibilities:
- Track and profile threat actors targeting BFSI and critical infrastructure in MENA region
- Produce tactical, operational, and strategic intelligence reports with MITRE ATT&CK mapping
- Monitor underground forums, Telegram channels, and dark web marketplaces for threat signals
- Develop threat actor TTPs, IOC sets, and YARA/Sigma detection rules
- Brief client CISOs and security teams on emerging threats
- Integrate threat feeds into client SIEM and SOAR platforms

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in cyber threat intelligence or security research
- GCTI or CREST CTI certification required
- GIAC GREM preferred for malware analysis component
- Proficiency with MITRE ATT&CK, STIX/TAXII, and MISP
- Experience with Recorded Future, Mandiant Advantage, or OpenCTI platforms
- Arabic language proficiency a strong advantage
- Python scripting for threat intelligence automation
