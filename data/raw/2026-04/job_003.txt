Title: Detection Engineer - SIEM
Company: Telstra Security
Location: Melbourne, VIC, Australia
Salary: AUD 120,000 - 150,000 annually
Domain: SOC
Source: Manual

Job Description:
Telstra Security is seeking a Detection Engineer to build and maintain a world-class detection content library across Microsoft Sentinel and Splunk, supporting managed security services for enterprise and government clients across Australia and Asia Pacific.

Responsibilities:
- Develop, test, and maintain detection rules in KQL and Splunk SPL aligned to MITRE ATT&CK
- Build SOAR playbooks in Microsoft Sentinel Automation and Cortex XSOAR for automated response
- Conduct purple team exercises with red team to validate and improve detection coverage
- Manage detection content lifecycle from requirements through deployment and tuning
- Onboard and normalise new log sources into the SIEM data platform
- Produce detection engineering documentation and coverage gap reports

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in detection engineering, SOC, or threat hunting
- Microsoft SC-200 certification required
- GCIA or Splunk Core Certified Power User preferred
- Expert KQL and Splunk SPL query writing skills
- Hands-on SOAR experience: Sentinel Automation or Cortex XSOAR
- Python for log parsing and automation scripting
- Familiarity with ASD Essential Eight and PSPF compliance frameworks
