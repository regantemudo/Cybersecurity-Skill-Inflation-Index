Title: Senior Penetration Tester - Red Team
Company: HackerOne
Location: Remote - Global
Salary: USD 130,000 - 165,000 annually
Domain: Penetration Testing
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-01

Job Description:
HackerOne is seeking a Senior Penetration Tester to join its internal red team, conducting full-scope adversary simulations against HackerOne's own infrastructure and validating vulnerability reports from the bug bounty community.

Responsibilities:
- Plan and execute full-scope red team operations targeting HackerOne's web, API, and cloud infrastructure
- Validate and reproduce vulnerability reports submitted through HackerOne's platform
- Develop custom exploitation tooling and bypass techniques for WAF, EDR, and MFA controls
- Conduct Active Directory and Azure AD attack path assessments
- Produce detailed red team reports with MITRE ATT&CK TTP mappings and remediation guidance
- Mentor junior penetration testers and review their technical outputs

Requirements:
- 6+ years experience in penetration testing or offensive security
- OSCP required; OSEP or OSWE strongly preferred
- Active bug bounty track record on major platforms
- Expert knowledge of web application, API, and cloud attack techniques
- Hands-on experience with Burp Suite Pro, BloodHound, Cobalt Strike, and Nuclei
- Python and Go scripting for custom tooling development
- CRTO or CRTE certification a strong advantage
