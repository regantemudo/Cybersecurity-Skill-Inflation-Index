Title: Vulnerability Researcher
Company: Trend Micro Research
Location: Singapore
Salary: SGD 9,000 - 13,000 per month
Domain: Penetration Testing
Source: Manual

Job Description:
Trend Micro Research is seeking a Vulnerability Researcher to discover and analyse zero-day vulnerabilities in operating systems, browsers, IoT firmware, and enterprise software, contributing to responsible disclosure programs and CVE publications.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct vulnerability research on Windows, Linux, macOS, and IoT firmware targets
- Perform binary analysis, reverse engineering, and fuzzing to identify security flaws
- Develop proof-of-concept exploits to demonstrate vulnerability impact
- Publish CVEs and contribute to responsible disclosure with vendors
- Present research at security conferences including Black Hat and DEF CON
- Collaborate with threat intelligence team to track exploitation in the wild

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in vulnerability research or exploit development
- OSCP required; OSED or OSEE strongly preferred
- Expert knowledge of x86/x64 assembly and binary analysis
- Proficiency with Ghidra, IDA Pro, Binary Ninja, and pwndbg
- Experience with fuzzing frameworks: AFL++, libFuzzer, or WinAFL
- C, C++, and Python programming for exploit development
- Track record of CVE discoveries preferred
