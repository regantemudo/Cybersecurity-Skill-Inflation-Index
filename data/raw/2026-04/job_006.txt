Salary: INR 12,00,000 - 20,00,000 annually

Job Description:
Wipro Cybersecurity Services is seeking a VAPT Engineer to conduct web application, mobile, and API security assessments for banking and telecom clients across India and the Middle East.

Responsibilities:
- Perform web application penetration testing covering OWASP Top 10 and business logic flaws
- Conduct Android and iOS mobile application assessments using Frida and Objection
- Perform REST and GraphQL API security testing
- Execute network infrastructure vulnerability assessments using Nessus and Nmap
- Produce detailed VAPT reports with risk-rated findings and remediation steps
- Provide remediation support and re-testing for client development teams

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in VAPT or application security testing
- CEH or eJPT certification required
- OSCP or eMAPT preferred
- Proficiency with Burp Suite Pro, Frida, MobSF, Nmap, and Nessus
- Knowledge of OWASP Top 10 and OWASP Mobile Top 10
- Python or Bash scripting for automation
- Understanding of CVSS scoring and risk classification
