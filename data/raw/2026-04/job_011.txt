Title: Mobile Security Engineer
Company: Samsung Research India
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: INR 25,00,000 - 40,00,000 annually
Domain: AppSec
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-06

Job Description:
Samsung Research India is seeking a Mobile Security Engineer to secure the Samsung Galaxy ecosystem including Android OS, Samsung Knox, and Galaxy applications serving 1+ billion devices globally.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct security assessments of Android applications, Knox container, and Samsung One UI components
- Perform static analysis using MobSF and Jadx and dynamic analysis using Frida and Objection
- Research and identify vulnerabilities in Android OS components and Samsung proprietary software
- Develop and maintain internal security testing tools and frameworks for Android
- Coordinate with Samsung Electronics Korea on vulnerability disclosure and patch development
- Represent Samsung at Android security community events and conferences
- Contribute to Samsung Mobile Security Rewards Program (SMRP) report triaging

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in mobile security or Android security research
- Deep knowledge of Android internals, Binder IPC, and SELinux policies
- Proficiency with Frida, MobSF, Jadx, and ADB for dynamic analysis
- GMOB or eMAPT certification required
- OSCP preferred
- Java, Kotlin, and C/C++ programming for Android security research
- Experience with Samsung Knox architecture preferred
- Track record of CVE discoveries in mobile platforms a strong advantage
