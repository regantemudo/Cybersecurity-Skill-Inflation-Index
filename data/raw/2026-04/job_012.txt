Title: Product Security Manager
Company: Grab
Location: Singapore
Salary: SGD 16,000 - 22,000 per month
Domain: AppSec
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-11

Job Description:
Grab is seeking a Product Security Manager to lead its application security program, overseeing threat modelling, vulnerability management, bug bounty operations, and security champions across Grab's super-app ecosystem serving 35 million+ users.

Responsibilities:
- Lead end-to-end application security program including SAST, DAST, SCA, and pen testing
- Build and manage Grab's public bug bounty program and coordinate with external researchers
- Define secure SDLC standards and enforce security gates across 500+ engineering squads
- Lead threat modelling sessions for Grab's fintech, mobility, and food delivery products
- Manage a team of 6 product security engineers across Singapore, Indonesia, and Vietnam
- Report program metrics and AppSec risk posture to VP Engineering and CISO quarterly
- Drive incident response for application-layer security incidents

Requirements:
- 8+ years experience in application security with at least 3 years in a management role
- CSSLP required; CISSP preferred
- Expert knowledge of OWASP Top 10, OWASP SAMM, and BSIMM framework
- Hands-on experience with Checkmarx, Snyk, and Burp Suite Enterprise
- Track record managing bug bounty programs on HackerOne or Bugcrowd
- Strong people management and executive stakeholder communication skills
- PCI-DSS and MAS TRM compliance knowledge preferred
