Job Description:
HSBC Technology India is seeking a SailPoint IdentityNow Engineer to implement and manage identity lifecycle automation for 250,000+ employees across 60+ countries, supporting HSBC's global identity governance transformation program.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement SailPoint IdentityNow sources, roles, policies, and access certifications
- Build joiner/mover/leaver automation workflows integrating Workday HR with Active Directory and Azure AD
- Develop SailPoint connectors for SAP, Salesforce, and custom applications
- Manage role-based access control (RBAC) and segregation of duties (SoD) controls
- Conduct quarterly access certification campaigns for SOX and ISO 27001 compliance
- Troubleshoot provisioning failures and maintain connector health
- Produce identity governance metrics and risk reporting for CISO dashboards

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience with SailPoint IdentityNow or IdentityIQ
- SailPoint Certified IdentityNow Engineer preferred
- Strong knowledge of SAML 2.0, OAuth 2.0, SCIM, and OIDC protocols
- Experience integrating IGA with Active Directory, Azure AD, and SAP
- CISSP or SC-300 certification preferred
- Java or BeanShell scripting for SailPoint customisation
- SOX, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance knowledge
