Deloitte Cyber Risk Singapore is seeking a CyberArk PAM Consultant to deliver privileged access management transformation programs for financial services and government clients across Southeast Asia.

- Lead CyberArk PAS deployments including Vault, PVWA, CPM, PSM, and PSMP

- Conduct PAM maturity assessments and produce client roadmap recommendations
- Configure CyberArk Secrets Manager for DevOps pipeline secrets management
- Support MAS TRM, PDPA, and MAS Notice 655 regulatory compliance requirements
- Train client administrators and produce platform documentation

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in CyberArk administration or PAM consulting
- CyberArk Defender required; CyberArk Sentry or Guardian preferred
- CISSP or CISM preferred
- Experience with CyberArk PAS, Secrets Manager, and Alero
- Knowledge of MAS TRM and Singapore regulatory compliance requirements
- PowerShell and REST API scripting for automation
- Strong client-facing communication and consulting skills
