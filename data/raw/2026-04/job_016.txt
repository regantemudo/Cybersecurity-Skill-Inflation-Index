Title: Senior GRC Analyst - Financial Services
Company: Citi
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Salary: INR 18,00,000 - 28,000,000 annually
Domain: GRC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-03

Job Description:
Citi is seeking a Senior GRC Analyst to manage information security risk and compliance programs across Citi's India operations, supporting RBI, SEBI, and global regulatory compliance under Citi's enterprise cybersecurity framework.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct cybersecurity risk assessments aligned to NIST CSF and ISO 27001 for technology platforms
- Manage regulatory compliance obligations under RBI IT Framework, SEBI CSCRF, and Citi's global standards
- Perform third-party vendor risk assessments and manage supplier security controls
- Lead internal security audits and coordinate responses to regulatory examination findings
- Maintain the information security risk register and report risk posture to senior management
- Develop and update security policies, standards, and procedures
- Support annual ISO 27001 surveillance audits and control evidence collection

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in GRC, IT audit, or information security risk management
- CISA certification required
- ISO 27001 Lead Auditor or Lead Implementer required
- CRISC or CISSP preferred
- Deep knowledge of RBI IT Framework and SEBI CSCRF regulatory requirements
- Experience with GRC platforms: ServiceNow GRC or Archer
- Strong report writing and stakeholder management skills
