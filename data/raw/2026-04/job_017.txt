Title: Cybersecurity Compliance Analyst - Healthcare
Company: Apollo Hospitals
Location: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
Salary: INR 10,00,000 - 16,00,000 annually
Domain: GRC
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-07

Job Description:
Apollo Hospitals is seeking a Cybersecurity Compliance Analyst to manage information security compliance across India's largest hospital network, ensuring alignment with HIPAA, India's DPDP Act, NABH, and internal security standards protecting patient health data.

Responsibilities:
- Manage HIPAA Security Rule compliance program across Apollo's 70+ hospital locations
- Conduct annual risk assessments aligned to HIPAA and India DPDP Act requirements
- Perform security assessments of medical device and clinical system vendors
- Maintain information security policy framework and ensure staff compliance training
- Support external audits by NABH and CERT-In empanelled auditors
- Track and remediate audit findings within agreed timelines
- Prepare compliance dashboards for CISO and Board reporting

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in IT compliance or GRC in healthcare or regulated sector
- CISA or ISO 27001 Lead Implementer certification required
- Knowledge of HIPAA Security Rule and India DPDP Act 2023
- CHPC (Certified Healthcare Privacy Compliance) preferred
- Experience with GRC tools: Vanta, Drata, or ServiceNow GRC
- Strong documentation and policy writing skills
- Familiarity with NABH accreditation requirements preferred
