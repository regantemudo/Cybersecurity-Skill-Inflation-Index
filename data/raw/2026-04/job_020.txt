Title: Firewall Security Engineer
Company: Emirates NBD
Location: Dubai, UAE
Salary: AED 22,000 - 32,000 per month
Domain: Network Security
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-08

Job Description:
Emirates NBD is seeking a Firewall Security Engineer to manage and optimise enterprise firewall infrastructure across UAE and international banking operations, covering Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, and Cisco environments.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and maintain Palo Alto Networks and Fortinet NGFW policies across 50+ firewalls
- Review and approve firewall change requests following ITIL change management process
- Conduct quarterly firewall rule base audits and cleanup of redundant access rules
- Implement network segmentation and micro-segmentation strategies
- Configure and manage Palo Alto Panorama for centralised policy management
- Respond to network security incidents and implement emergency firewall rule changes
- Ensure compliance with CBUAE and UAE IA cybersecurity regulatory requirements

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in firewall management or network security operations
- PCNSE (Palo Alto Networks Certified Network Security Engineer) required
- Fortinet NSE 7 or CCNP Security preferred
- Hands-on Palo Alto Panorama and Fortinet FortiManager experience
- Knowledge of CBUAE cybersecurity regulations and UAE IA standards
- Understanding of network protocols: BGP, OSPF, MPLS, and VPN
- CISSP preferred for senior candidates
