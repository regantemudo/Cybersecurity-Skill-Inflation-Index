Job Description:
Infosys is seeking a Network Security Architect to design and lead Zero Trust network transformation programs for global enterprise clients, replacing perimeter-based security with identity-aware micro-segmented architectures.

Responsibilities:
- Design enterprise Zero Trust network architecture aligned to NIST SP 800-207 and CISA ZT Maturity Model
- Lead SASE architecture designs integrating Zscaler, Palo Alto Prisma, or Netskope
- Architect microsegmentation strategies using Illumio, Guardicore, or VMware NSX-T
- Produce detailed network security architecture documentation and HLD/LLD designs
- Conduct network security maturity assessments and produce transformation roadmaps
- Present architecture proposals to CISO, CTO, and executive stakeholders
- Lead a team of 4 network security engineers on client delivery engagements

Requirements:
- 8+ years experience in network security with 3+ years in architecture roles
- CCIE Security or PCNSE required
- CISSP required; CCSP preferred
- Deep knowledge of SASE, ZTNA, SD-WAN, and microsegmentation
- Expert knowledge of Zscaler, Palo Alto Prisma Access, or Netskope SASE
- Experience with NIST SP 800-207 Zero Trust implementation
- Strong executive presentation and consulting delivery skills
