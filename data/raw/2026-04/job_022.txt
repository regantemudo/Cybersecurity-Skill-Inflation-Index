Title: Digital Forensics Analyst

Salary: SGD 8,000 - 12,000 per month

Job Description:
Deloitte Forensics Singapore is seeking a Digital Forensics Analyst to support corporate investigations, regulatory inquiries, and litigation support engagements for financial services and government clients across Asia Pacific.

Responsibilities:
- Acquire and preserve digital evidence from endpoints, servers, cloud storage, and mobile devices
- Perform forensic analysis using EnCase, Axiom, and FTK across Windows, macOS, and Linux systems
- Conduct eDiscovery processing and review using Nuix and Relativity platforms
- Support regulatory investigations and produce court-admissible forensic reports
- Analyse mobile device data from iOS and Android using Cellebrite UFED
- Coordinate chain of custody documentation and evidence handling procedures
- Present forensic findings to legal teams and regulatory bodies

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in digital forensics or eDiscovery
- GCFE or GCFA certification required
- EnCase Certified Examiner (EnCE) or IACIS CFCE preferred
- Proficiency with EnCase, Axiom Cyber, FTK, and Cellebrite UFED
- Experience with eDiscovery platforms: Nuix or Relativity
- Knowledge of legal hold procedures and chain of custody requirements
- Understanding of Singapore CPC and MAS regulatory investigation requirements
