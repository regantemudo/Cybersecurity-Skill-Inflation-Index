Title: Malware Reverse Engineer
Company: Kaspersky GReAT
Location: Remote - India
Salary: INR 24,00,000 - 40,00,000 annually

Job Description:
Kaspersky Global Research and Analysis Team (GReAT) is seeking a Malware Reverse Engineer to analyse sophisticated nation-state and cybercrime malware, contributing to Kaspersky's world-leading threat intelligence research published globally.

Responsibilities:
- Perform advanced static and dynamic malware analysis on APT and cybercrime malware samples
- Reverse engineer malware using IDA Pro, Ghidra, and x64dbg for deep technical understanding
- Analyse C2 protocols, evasion techniques, and persistence mechanisms
- Develop YARA and Sigma detection rules from malware analysis findings
- Write technical research reports and blog posts on APT campaigns
- Present findings at security conferences including Virus Bulletin and HITB
- Contribute to Kaspersky Threat Intelligence Portal with new threat actor profiles

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in malware analysis or threat intelligence research
- GREM (GIAC Reverse Engineering Malware) required
- GCFA or GCFE preferred
- Expert knowledge of x86/x64 assembly and Windows internals
- Proficiency with IDA Pro, Ghidra, x64dbg, and PE analysis tools
- Python scripting for malware automation and YARA rule development
- Track record of published security research or CVE discoveries preferred
- Deep understanding of APT TTPs and nation-state threat actor methodologies
