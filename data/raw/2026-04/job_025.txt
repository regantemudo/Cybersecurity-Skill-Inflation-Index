Title: OT Cybersecurity Specialist
Company: Schneider Electric
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: INR 22,00,000 - 35,00,000 annually
Domain: OT/ICS Security
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-04

Job Description:
Schneider Electric is seeking an OT Cybersecurity Specialist to secure industrial control systems across manufacturing, energy, and data centre customers in India and South Asia, delivering IEC 62443-aligned security programs.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct OT/ICS security assessments using IEC 62443 Zone and Conduit methodology
- Design and implement network segmentation between IT and OT environments using DMZ architecture
- Deploy and configure Claroty and Schneider EcoStruxure cybersecurity monitoring
- Perform vulnerability assessments on Schneider PLCs, HMIs, and SCADA components
- Develop OT security policies, incident response playbooks, and operator training programs
- Support customers through IEC 62443 Level 2 compliance certification programs
- Conduct pre-sales technical engagements and produce OT security proposals

Requirements:
- 4+ years experience in OT/ICS security or industrial automation
- GICSP (Global Industrial Cyber Security Professional) required
- IEC 62443 Cybersecurity Professional certification preferred
- Hands-on experience with Claroty, Nozomi, or similar OT monitoring platforms
- Knowledge of Modbus, DNP3, EtherNet/IP, and Profinet industrial protocols
- Electrical engineering or automation background preferred
- CISSP or CISM preferred for senior candidates
