Title: SCADA Security Analyst
Company: Singapore Power Group
Location: Singapore
Salary: SGD 7,500 - 11,000 per month
Domain: OT/ICS Security
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-09

Job Description:
Singapore Power Group is seeking a SCADA Security Analyst to monitor and protect industrial control systems across Singapore's electricity transmission and gas distribution networks, supporting CSA Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) compliance.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor OT/SCADA environments using Dragos Platform for threat detection and anomaly identification
- Conduct vulnerability assessments of PLCs, RTUs, and SCADA workstations
- Implement and manage OT network segmentation using Purdue Model architecture
- Develop and maintain OT-specific incident response playbooks
- Support Singapore Cybersecurity Act (CSA) and NERC CIP compliance requirements
- Coordinate with IT security team on IT/OT convergence security architecture
- Participate in CSA's operational technology security exercises

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in OT/ICS security or SCADA operations
- GICSP certification required
- IEC 62443 foundation training preferred
- Hands-on Dragos Platform, Claroty, or Nozomi experience
- Understanding of DNP3, Modbus, and IEC 61850 protocols
- Knowledge of Singapore Cybersecurity Act and CSA CII framework
- CISSP or CompTIA Security+ preferred
