Title: Privacy Engineer - Platform
Company: Swiggy
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
Salary: INR 18,00,000 - 30,00,000 annually

Job Description:
Swiggy is seeking a Privacy Engineer to build privacy-by-design capabilities into its food delivery and quick commerce platform, ensuring technical compliance with India's DPDP Act and GDPR for international markets serving 100 million+ users.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement technical privacy controls including data minimisation, pseudonymisation, and encryption
- Build and maintain data classification engine using Microsoft Purview and BigID across Swiggy's data lake
- Implement consent management platform integrated with Swiggy's mobile apps and web platform
- Develop automated data retention and deletion pipelines across production databases
- Perform privacy engineering reviews for new features processing sensitive personal data
- Build privacy compliance dashboards for DPO and leadership reporting
- Integrate DLP controls to prevent personal data leakage across Swiggy's data platforms

Requirements:
- 3+ years experience in privacy engineering or data governance
- CIPP/E certification required; CIPT preferred
- Hands-on experience with Microsoft Purview, BigID, or Varonis
- Python or SQL for data discovery and privacy automation
- Knowledge of India DPDP Act 2023 and GDPR technical requirements
- Understanding of data classification and labelling frameworks
- Experience with consent management platforms: OneTrust or Usercentrics
