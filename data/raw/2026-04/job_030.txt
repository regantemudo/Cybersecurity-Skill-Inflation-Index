Title: Chief Privacy Officer (CPO)
Company: Nykaa (FSN E-Commerce Ventures)
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Salary: INR 60,00,000 - 100,00,000 annually
Domain: Data Privacy
Source: Manual
Collected: 2026-04-15

Job Description:
Nykaa is seeking a Chief Privacy Officer to establish and lead Nykaa's enterprise privacy program, driving DPDP Act compliance across India's leading beauty and fashion e-commerce platform processing personal data of 200 million+ registered customers.

Responsibilities:
- Establish and lead Nykaa's enterprise privacy governance framework
- Serve as primary liaison with India's Data Protection Board under DPDP Act 2023
- Lead enterprise-wide data mapping, inventory, and classification program
- Build and manage a team of 5 privacy professionals across legal, engineering, and compliance
- Oversee privacy-by-design review for all new Nykaa product launches and features
- Manage regulatory inquiries, data breach notifications, and data subject rights at scale
- Report privacy risk posture to Board of Directors and Executive Leadership Team
- Coordinate cross-border data transfer compliance for Nykaa's international operations

Requirements:
- 10+ years experience in data privacy leadership with 3+ years in CPO or DPO role
- CIPP/E and CIPM certifications required; CIPT preferred
- Law degree (LLB or LLM) with data privacy specialisation strongly preferred
- Expert knowledge of India DPDP Act 2023, GDPR, and CCPA
- Experience with privacy management platforms: OneTrust at enterprise scale
- Strong Board and executive communication capability
- Track record leading data breach incident response and regulatory notifications
- CISSP or CISM preferred for technical credibility
